## Priority Issues

[P1] Work cards look fully interactive (hover scale, sliding arrow, title shift) but have no href/click handler anywhere — confirmed in script.js. For a visitor vetting credibility, clicking into a project is high-intent and silently fails. Fix: either ship minimal case-study pages now, or strip the hover-affordance cues until real destinations exist. Suggested command: /impeccable craft (case study pages) or /impeccable harden (remove false affordance) — direction question for the user.

[P2] Alt text repeats the same generic template ("[Name] project by Omiko") six times, less specific than the rest of the site's voice. Suggested command: /impeccable clarify.

[P3] Grid/List toggle is desktop-only (hidden below 1024px) and the two views look nearly identical even on desktop. Flagged as a question, not a defect.
