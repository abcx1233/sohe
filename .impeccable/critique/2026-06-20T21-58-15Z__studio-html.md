## Anti-Patterns Verdict

## Overall Impression

Bones are right (restrained type, grey system, consistent nav/footer) but the page under-delivers on its own premise twice: no team in the Team section, and the Services section breaks the site's entire color discipline.

## What's Working
- Voice consistency in most copy ("small, dedicated team that cares about getting the details right").
- Layout restraint — no icon-grid filler or stat-block clichés.
- Consistent header/footer/nav system with the rest of the site.

## Priority Issues

[P1] The "Team" section has no team in it. Eyebrow says "Team," headline claims a dedicated team, then nothing follows — no photos, names, or roles. Contradicts PRODUCT.md's "craft is the argument" principle on the one page meant to build trust in the people doing the work. Fix: add real team content (photos/names/roles or a specific paragraph) in the existing voice. Suggested command: /impeccable craft (studio team section).

[P1] Services-cloud's six saturated colors (pink/green/yellow/orange/blue/magenta) break the site's two-tone + grey ramp system — already flagged in DESIGN.md as an inconsistency, not a sanctioned pattern. Lands on the page meant to reinforce "quiet confidence." Fix: bring tags into the existing system (weight/size differentiation or grey ramp) instead of six hues. Suggested command: /impeccable quieter (services-cloud tags).

[P2] No closing CTA after Services section, unlike index.html's "Let's talk shop" CTA. Misses a conversion moment right after the page's strongest credibility beat. Suggested command: /impeccable craft (closing CTA).

[P2] Six cloud tags carry tabindex="0" with no click handler/href/role (confirmed: no listener in script.js) — keyboard/screen-reader users get six functionless tab stops next to the one tag that is functional ("Contact us"). Suggested command: /impeccable audit (accessibility pass on studio.html).

[P3] "We measure success by your results" reads as filler vs. the site's specific voice elsewhere ("Simple packages. No surprises."). Suggested command: /impeccable clarify.
