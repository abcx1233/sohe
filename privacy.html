Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

Omiko ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you contact us through this website, and how it's used.

What we collect

When you submit the contact form on this site, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Details about your project that you choose to share with us

How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry and discuss your project with you. We don't use it for marketing, don't sell it, and don't share it with anyone except as described below.

Third-party processing

Form submissions are processed by Web3Forms, a third-party form-handling service, which delivers your message to our inbox. Web3Forms may briefly process your data as part of this delivery. We don't control Web3Forms' own data practices directly; you can review their privacy policy at web3forms.com for more detail.

How long we keep it

We retain enquiry details only as long as needed to respond to you and, if we go on to work together, for the duration of that relationship plus a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to tell you what information we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. Contact us at hello@omiko.co.uk to make any of these requests.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email hello@omiko.co.uk.
